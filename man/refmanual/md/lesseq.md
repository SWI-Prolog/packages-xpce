# class lesseq {#class-lesseq}

Conditional expression.  Evaluates both left and right sides and compare
the result.  See class binary_condition for details.

@see topic Conditions


## Send methods {#class-lesseq-send}

- lesseq->_execute
    Evaluate <-left and <-right to a numeric value and succeed of <-left
    is less then or equal to <-right.
